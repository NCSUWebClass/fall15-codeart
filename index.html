PROJECT LENT

Turning library technology into art.

LENT is a pie and graphical visualization of a few devices available for checkout in Hunt Library. On the left is the pie chart, where the circumference represents the total amount of devices, and the amount each arc is filled up represents the amount of items checked out. On the right is a more visual representation, with each device being represented by its own shape shown in the legend. Each device will also emanate a sound based upon its position on the screen.
